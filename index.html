The person behind the magic

Hi, I'm Guy — The Party Guy

I've practised magic since I was 18, when my great uncle — who used to entertain me for hours with his tricks — left me all of his magic books and props. I took it on myself to carry on his legacy.

At 25 I had the idea (which plenty called madness) to entertain children with my magic, and I've never looked back. I've since performed at hundreds of parties and events, from small and intimate to enormous and gloriously chaotic — tailoring each one so everyone has the best time, children and adults alike.
